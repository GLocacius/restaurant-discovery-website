About

Local Eats & Hidden Gems was created by passionate food lovers who wanted to shine a light on the unique, independently owned restaurants that give our community its flavor.

By supporting these small businesses, we help keep money in the local economy, preserve culinary diversity, and strengthen connections between neighbors.

About Local Eats & Hidden Gems

Charlotte Local Eats and Hidden Gems is a website designed to expand our community's food palate and explore unique restaurants in our city Charlotte Local Eats and Hidden Gems .

Copyright © Gage Locacius September 14, 2025

Interactive Map

The interactive map will display local restaurants with pinpoint markers, allowing users to explore dining options by location. Each marker will provide details such as the restaurant's name, hours, and contact information.

American Cuisine

The American cuisine section will highlight restaurants that specialize in classic and modern American dishes. Users will see options ranging from casual diners and burger spots to upscale steakhouses and contemporary eateries.

Asian Cuisine

The Asian cuisine section will feature restaurants offering a wide variety of dishes from cultures such as Chinese, Japanese, Thai, and more. Users can explore everything from sushi bars and noodle shops to authentic family-style restaurants.

Seafood

The seafood section will showcase restaurants that specialize in fresh and flavorful seafood dishes. Users can explore options ranging from casual fish shacks and oyster bars to upscale seafood dining experiences.

Gluten Free

The gluten-free section will highlight restaurants that offer dedicated gluten-free menus or a wide selection of gluten-free options. Users can browse everything from bakeries and cafes to full service restaurants that accommodate dietary needs.

Contact Us

The Contact Us section invites users to share their favorite dining spots with us. We encourage visitors to submit restaurant suggestions so we can feature them on the website and expand our community's food discovery. Please include details like the restaurants name, location, cuisine type, and why it deserves to be highlighted.
